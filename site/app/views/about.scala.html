Damage Tracker

Damage Tracker is a software system for capturing and managing tornado damage information. A mobile application for Android allows users to take geotagged photos of damage scene. Within the app these images can be annotated with descriptions and EF ratings. Images are then uploaded to this web application, where they can be stored and viewed by an online community of researchers and the public. From this site images can be edited, categorized into collections, and visualized on an interactive web map. The goal of this system is to simplify data sharing after natural disasters, which reduces duplicate collection effort, encourages more collaboration, and has the potential to improve the quality and depth of further research.
